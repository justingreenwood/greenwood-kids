Perry

About

      Perry was human born in Justinia a place near Avelon of the elf kingdom. His father worked hard making magic slates work faster and more efficiently. His mother was a busy body at home. She took care of the farm, the house, and all five of the kids. Perry went to Shiriana's school for the weak humans. When Perry was a young man, he went to Avelon University to learn his fathers trade. He is currently working as a fastfood worker at Swampling's.
